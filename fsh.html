The FSH Women's Club is an active group of students and staff that work extensively conducting events in the College of Science and Humanities. Various events,competitions,workshops and programs are conducted and various stalls are put throughout the year to educate and spread awareness about women empowerment and draw attention towards the issues faced by women in our society.
